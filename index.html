Social Sentiments

A Chrome extension that informs you of how much positive and negative Facebook posts you consume.


or go to chrome web store








Facebook Fuels Negativity

The site can easily put you in bad mood as the negative posts on Facebook are contagious.
Thankfully, the opposite can be said about positive posts: see an upbeat update,
and you're more likely to feel like smiling yourself.














Bigger Picture

See an overview to tell you how much negativity surrounds you.









Timeline

A day by day breakdown of the posts you were presented.







Privacy

All the sentiment analysis is done locally using the awesome sentiment library.
No personally identifiable data is sent to any server.
